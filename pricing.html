Frequently asked questions
How does the 1M token limit on Free work? ▼
The free plan tracks up to 1 million tokens per month across all your AI API calls combined. This resets on the 1st of each month. If you exceed it, tracking pauses until the next reset — your app keeps running, we just stop counting.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, completely. Cancel from Settings → Billing at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
Does the SDK slow down my app? ▼
No. The SDK wraps your fetch calls asynchronously and batches events in the background. The overhead is under 1ms per call in benchmarks. Your users will never notice it.
What counts as a "token"? ▼
We track the same token counts reported by each AI provider in their API response. Input and output tokens are both counted. Our token estimation matches what you are billed for by the provider.
Is my API data secure? ▼
Yes. We never store the content of your prompts or responses — only metadata (token counts, latency, model name, cost). Your actual AI inputs and outputs never leave your app. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
Do you offer a free trial on paid plans? ▼
Yes — Pro and Team plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only get charged when the trial ends and you choose to continue.
